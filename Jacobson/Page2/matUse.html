Pokemon Game Explained

The Playing Field

This is what the board should look like before the game starts. When everything is set, have the card on the left side of the board. The special cards then flip the card in the middle and the two cards on the bottom. So, both players know the Pokémon that are in play.

How to Draw and Discard

The draw pile must have 60 cards. The player must draw a basis when they draw their starting seven cards. If the player doesn't draw a basic Pokémon, then they must show the other player their hands. Then they put those into the draw pile and reshuffle. They keep on doing this until they get a basic Pokémon. However, many times it takes the player; the other player gets to take the same number of cards as the number of tries it took. The player will draw a card at the beginning of each turn. When a player runs out of cards in the draw pile, the player will lose the game. When a player damages the Pokémon's health, they put the Pokémon, and anything that is attached to it, also goes in the discard pile. Then any trainer cards that is one use goes into the discard pile also.

How to use the Bench

These Pokémon cards must be basic Pokémon. The player can only have a total of 5 basic Pokémon in the bench. When a Pokémon from the center retreats into the bench, then one of the Pokémon must move into attack. The bench Pokémon can use an ability while being in the bench area to attack Pokémon on the attack. Bench Pokémon can also earn energy cards. If a Pokémon gets damaged and retreats, they keep the damage that it has taken into the bench. When a player doesn't have any more Pokémon in the bench to move up to attack. When they only have one Pokémon lefty that is attacking. Then that Pokémon get knocked out, and the player loses the game.

Prize Area

After having the bench and the attacking Pokémon set and ready. The player will grab the top 6 Pokémon cards in their prize area. When a player knocks out a plyer Pokémon, then they can randomly pick one of their prize cards. The first player gets all of their prize cards, then that player will win the game.
